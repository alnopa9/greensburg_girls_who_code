Connect

On this page, our aim is to build a connect page that allows your site's visitors to easily access your other social media pages; like GitHub, Google, LinkedIn, Twitter, or Instagram. We want the logos of each platform to easily differentiate between them, to do this we could go to each site and save its logo, or we can use libraries! For this example we will be using libraries to lighten the amount of code that our computers parse when loading the page.
